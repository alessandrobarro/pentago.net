Ready for a twist?

Pentago is a fast-paced strategy game where players aim to create a row of five marbles before their opponent can. The twist? After placing each marble, you'll have to twist one of the game's quadrants. Join a game now!







"An award winning concept"


First invented by Tomas Flodén, the game was developed and commercialized in North-America by MindtwisterUSA, which preserves all the credits. Pentago has been recognized as Game of the Year in Sweden and France, between 2005 and 2006. Notably, in this last year, Pentago was accredited the Mensa Mind Games award. This platform was created by a sincere fan of the game's philosophy, in hopes of spreading it's notoriety among the next generations.
